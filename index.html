about me

I am a software developer in Ann Arbor, MI. Since starting in software consulting in 2012, I have worked with several clients on projects in several technologies including AngularJS, EmberJS, DropWizard, Django, and Android.

As a youngster, I was fascinated with the workings of computers, and have not stopped learning about them since. I am also a mentor for FIRST robotics team 3322.
